Goal:
Add live lineup changes, playing time tracking, yellow cards, red cards, and red-card restrictions.

Implement these slices:
- Phase 7 substitutions and playing time.
- Phase 8 cards and red card behavior.
- Substitution modal/flow.
- Outgoing active field player selection.
- Incoming eligible bench player selection.
- Current lineup update.
- Bench update.
- Playing time tracking in seconds.
- Yellow card event route and UI.
- Red card event route and UI.
- Sent-off state.
- Red-card removal from field.
- Red-card restrictions.

Substitution rules:
- Only active field players can be substituted off.
- Only eligible bench players can be substituted on.
- A sent-off player cannot be substituted on.
- Playing time stops for the outgoing player.
- Playing time starts for the incoming player.
- A player cannot exist twice on the field.
- Substitution appears in the timeline.
- Multi-entity substitution writes must be transactional.

Card rules:
- Yellow cards appear in the timeline and do not remove the player.
- Red-carded player leaves the field.
- Red-carded player cannot return.
- Red-carded player cannot take penalties.
- Red-carded player cannot be substituted back in.
- Field player count is reduced by one.
- Playing time stops for a red-carded player.

Authorization and security:
- Live event writes must be authorized server-side.
- Write routes require CSRF.
- Validation and domain-state checks must precede writes.

UI requirements:
- Live controls remain mobile-first and contextual.
- Red card should require deliberate confirmation because it changes lineup availability.
- Timeline clearly distinguishes substitutions, yellow cards, and red cards.

Explicitly do not implement yet:
- Penalties during match unless needed only to respect red-card eligibility.
- Extra time.
- Penalty shootout UI.
- Livestream.
- Finished-match corrections.
- Ratings/training/statistics expansion.

Acceptance criteria:
- Coach can substitute players during an active match.
- Lineup and bench update immediately after server success.
- Playing time is stored in seconds.
- Player cannot exist twice on field.
- Invalid outgoing/incoming choices are rejected.
- Coach can register yellow card.
- Coach can register red card.
- Red-carded player leaves field and cannot return.
- Red-carded player cannot be selected as an incoming substitute.
- Red card appears in timeline.
- Tests cover SU-01 through SU-04, RC-01 through RC-03, lineup duplicate prevention, playing-time start/stop behavior, unauthorized writes, and CSRF rejection.
